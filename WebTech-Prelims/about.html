ABOUT US

Healthy Horizons is a website that aims to spread awareness about sustainable development goal 3: good health and well-being. We aim to promote the well-being of people of all ages, aligning with the United Nations Agenda 2030 for sustainable development. The website also highlights progress made to ensure that the targets proposed in the SDG are met by the year 2030. Here at Healthy Horizons, you can find articles about SDG 3 and the steps made to address each goal in the Philippines.


Our group, BritneyBlitz, believes that access to quality healthcare is a basic human right. Taking care of one’s health is important to help achieve other sustainable development goals, such as access to affordable clean energy and promoting sustainable economic growth. Our website provides information about SDG 3 and the proposed targets and progress made to ensure every person has access to quality healthcare in the Philippines.


Join us in spreading awareness about good health and well-being to build a healthier world for everyone. Together, we can ensure no one is left behind in the goal of good health and well-being.
